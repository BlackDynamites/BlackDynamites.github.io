About Us

Established in 2007, Utour China has a team of professional itinerary planners and operators, as well as our own experienced and proficient English, French, Spanish, German, Russian and Italian speaking tour guides.

Every year we welcome over 40,000 guests from Europe, Australia and the Americas to Shanghai and its surroundings. During our longstanding involvement in tourism, we built up a outstanding reputation and lasting partnerships with many famous travel agencies in China and overseas.

When traveling with Utour China you get:

The best products

All our products only include the most beautiful scenery combined with in-depth knowledge.

Lean operations

We have strict internal position settings and process arrangements, and we have clear quality standards for each position to ensure a high level of hospitality.

Close to the customer

We understand the individual needs of mature customers, and provide professional tracking services according to the different situations of each customer to meet the needs of customers.

Leading price

We have world-class partners at home and abroad, stable flow has achieved unified procurement, maximizing cost reduction and providing customers with competitive prices.

Utour China creates the greatest travel value for you!
